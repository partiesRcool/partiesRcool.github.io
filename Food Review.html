Food Review: Spring Rolls

by partiesRcool

"Dam, this shit good!"

I remember the very moment I heard the the good news from the Right Honourable Twitter User, @katexyl, also known by the name “👨🏻‍🚀.” Upon requesting to this mutual, “PMO 🤞” I was informed that I would be receiving provisions to be made by her the following day. This is my reaction:

Ian gon hol u, it took me a minute to unwrap the first one, likely due to my lacking motor skills. I asked 👨🏻‍🚀 if I should put the provided crushed peanuts in the peanut sauce, to which she gave an affirmative response. The peanut sauce was masterful. I fw peanuts, so I was like “dam dis hella good.” The spring rolls were good as well—I enjoy consuming shrimp. At the conclusion of my consuming two out of the four spring rolls, I felt satiated and content.

A Felon, Redeemed

While this might be the same person responsible for the infamous Milpitas Massacre, I believe that the integrity of the food, paired with its ability to quickly satisfy one’s hunger, makes the mitigating factors outweigh the aggravating circumstances. This leads me to the final verdict:

FINAL RATING: Not Guilty/10
